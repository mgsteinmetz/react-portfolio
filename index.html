About Me

Hello! I am currently a student at University of Minnesota Coding Bootcamps. I also do comercial, and residential lawn mowing for Murtha Lawn Care LLC. I grew up about forty minutes east of the twin cities, in the small town of Hammond, WI. Outside of work and the classrom I enjoy many different activities such as; hunting, fishing, watching sports, and playing video games. The process of starting my developer career is not a short one. I came out of high school thinking that I was going to play college football for the University of Wisconsin-River Falls. I quickly realized that was not the route I wanted. I went on to change my major from Environmental Engineering, to Physical Therapy. I tansferred schools, and enrolled at Chippewa Valley Technical College. After leaving CVTC I was in search of a new career path, and my older brother came to me with the idea of becoming a Software Engineer like him. I never thought of the tech field as being where I would land, however I really enjoy technology and video games so it didn't seem out of reach. After researching more about web developpment, and learning to use some software on my own. I realized that I actually really enjoyed working with the different software programs!
